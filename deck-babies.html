The honest part

It's not that I don't like them. I just don't trust myself with them.

They're small. They're loud. They have no neck strength. And everyone watches you like you're defusing a bomb. The pressure is real. The fear is irrational. Both things are true at the same time.

One day I'll be ready. Today I'll just wave.
